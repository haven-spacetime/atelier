# Task: Shopmonkey CRM Analysis & Alternatives

## Objective
Deep dive into Shopmonkey's CRM features and find alternatives suitable for a luxury auto shop with ~$60K/month revenue.

## Context
- **Target user:** Atelier Motors (luxury/exotic car wrapping, 6 employees, $60K/mo)
- **Current CRM:** Trello
- **Preferred CRM:** Shopmonkey (but too expensive at $500/mo)
- **Budget:** One-time fee of $6,000-7,500 for custom solution

## Research Tasks

### 1. Shopmonkey Deep Dive
Visit these URLs and document features:
- https://www.shopmonkey.io/product-tour/crm
- https://www.shopmonkey.io/product-tour/overview
- https://www.shopmonkey.io/product-tour/payments

Screenshot key screens. Document:
- Core CRM features (customer management, job tracking, scheduling)
- Shop management features (inventory, workflow, team assignments)
- Payment/invoice features
- Reporting/analytics
- Mobile app capabilities
- Pricing tiers

### 2. Alternative Solutions Research
Find 5-7 alternatives to Shopmonkey for auto shop CRM:
- AutoFluent
- Shop-Ware
- Mitchell 1
- ARI
- Auto Repair Bill
- RepairShopr
- Others

For each, document:
- Pricing (monthly, per user)
- Key features
- Pros/cons for luxury auto shop
- Suitability for small-medium shop (6-10 employees)

### 3. Gap Analysis
What does Shopmonkey do that Trello doesn't?
What specific features would Atelier Motors need?

## Deliverables
1. Screenshots of Shopmonkey features (saved to `workspace/outputs/shopmonkey/`)
2. Comparison spreadsheet/markdown of alternatives
3. Product requirements document (PRD) for a custom CRM tailored to Atelier Motors

## Output Files
- `workspace/memory/research/shopmonkey-analysis.md`
- `workspace/memory/research/crm-alternatives.md`
- `workspace/outputs/shopmonkey-screenshots/` (directory with images)
